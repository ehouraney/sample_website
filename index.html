One-Pot Chicken and Rice

How to spice up an everyday meal.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Basmati Rice
  • Chickpeas
  • Onion
  • Chicken Broth
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Montreal Chicken Seasoning
  • Radia - Complete Seasoning
  • Chili Powder
  • Zdan - 7 Spice
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Parsley Flakes

Instructions:

  1. Prepare and season chicken breast with olive oil, salt, cumin, paprika, montreal chicken seasoning, complete seasoning, chilli powder, 7 spice, and ground cinnamon (to taste)
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook the chicken half way on high heat (sear both sides evenly)
  3. Once cooked half way, remove from pan and place to side
  4. In the same pan, saute chopped onion
  5. Add chickpeas and rinsed rice (recipe card shows two cups)
  6. Lightly toast rice and chickpeas, then add three cups of chicken stock
  7. Add cinnamon, salt, cumin and paprika into rice, mix
  8. Add chicken from earlier into the pan, place lid onto pan
  9. Let rice and chicken cook on high heat until boiled, then lower on medium heat for 15 minutes
  10. Top with parsley flakes and enjoy!

Dubai Chocolate

Make your own of this viral Middle Eastern sweet!

Ingredients:

  • Gharadelhi Melting Chocolate
  • Shredded Katafeh
  • Pistachio Butter
  • Tahini
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Melt two bags of melting chocolate over a double boiler
  2. Pour thin layer of chocolate into a chocolate mold (enough to create bottom layer)
  3. Refridgerate undtil hardened
  4. In a pan, melt some butter and litghtly toast shredded katafeh until golden
  5. Once toasted, add in bowl and mix with pistachio butter and a little but of tahini
  6. Take mold out of fridge, place a small amount of the katafeh/pistachio/tahini mix into each mold
  7. With the rest of your melting chocolate, pour a layer over the stuffed molds
  8. Place in fridge to cool
  9. Add drizzle of chcolate, tahini or pistachio on top of each mold (optional)
  10. Pop out of mold and enjoy!

Fresh Salad

A light and healthy salad with fresh veggies.

Ingredients:

  • Lettuce - 1 head
  • Tomato - 1
  • Cucumber - 1
  • Olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Vinegar - 1 tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper - to taste

Instructions:

  1. Chop all vegetables and place them in a large bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the veggies, toss, and serve!
