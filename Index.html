Pancakes

Fluffy and delicious pancakes for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients:

  • Flour - 1 cup
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Egg - 1
  • Baking powder - 1 tsp
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Maple Syrup - for serving

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients into a smooth batter.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan and pour batter in small circles.
  3. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
  4. Serve with butter and maple syrup.

Fresh Salad

A light and healthy salad with fresh veggies.

Ingredients:

  • Lettuce - 1 head
  • Tomato - 1
  • Cucumber - 1
  • Olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Vinegar - 1 tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper - to taste

Instructions:

  1. Chop all vegetables and place them in a large bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the veggies, toss, and serve!
